### 2. About Gumi Save

Gumi Save is a **household budget recording application** that helps you track income, expenses, savings goals, and budget allocations. The App is designed primarily for Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) and Filipino families.

**What Gumi Save IS:**
- A personal household budget and savings tracking tool
- A self-reporting financial diary app
- A receipt scanning tool (using OCR to pre-fill expense records from photos)
