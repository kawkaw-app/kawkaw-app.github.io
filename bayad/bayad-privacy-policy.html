Your privacy is fully protected.

BAYAD does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Everything you enter stays on your device — always. There are no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

01

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the BAYAD mobile application ("App") available on Google Play Store, developed for users in the Philippines. BAYAD is a utility bill tracker that helps Filipino households estimate and monitor electricity, water, and internet expenses.

Because the App operates entirely on your device with no server connection, this policy is straightforward: we do not collect any data about you.

02

Information We Do Not Collect

BAYAD does not collect any of the following:

  • ✕ Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, address)
  • ✕ Device identifiers (device ID, advertising ID, IMEI)
  • ✕ Location data (GPS, IP address, region)
  • ✕ Usage analytics or crash reports
  • ✕ Financial or billing account information
  • ✕ Photos, contacts, or any other device data
  • ✕ Any data entered into the App
No account or login is required. You can use BAYAD immediately after installation without providing any personal information.
03

How the App Stores Data

Any data you input (such as estimated consumption or region selection) is saved using local device storage only (AsyncStorage). This data:

  • ✓ Never leaves your device
  • ✓ Is not accessible to the developer or any third party
  • ✓ Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App
  • ✓ Can be cleared at any time through your device settings
04

Internet & Third-Party Services

BAYAD works fully offline. The App does not make any network requests and does not communicate with any external server, API, or third-party service.

No third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or social login are integrated into the App.

The App does not require an internet connection at any point during normal use. Utility rate data is bundled within the App and updated through App Store updates only.
05

Children's Privacy

BAYAD does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since the App collects no data whatsoever, it poses no privacy risk to users of any age.

06

Philippine Data Privacy Act

BAYAD is designed in full compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) and its Implementing Rules and Regulations, as enforced by the National Privacy Commission (NPC) of the Philippines.

Because no personal information is collected, processed, or stored by the developer, there is no data processing activity subject to regulation under RA 10173.

07

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions or Concerns?
