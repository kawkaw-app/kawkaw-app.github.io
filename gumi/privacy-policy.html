Introduction

Welcome to Gumi Save ("App," "we," "us," or "our"), a personal household budgeting application operated by KawKaw.dev. We are committed to protecting your personal information in full compliance with:

  • NPC Circular No. 2022-01 — Guidelines on Administrative Fines
  • NPC Circular No. 2022-04 — Registration of Data Processing Systems and Designation of DPO
  • NPC Circular No. 2023-04 — Guidelines on Consent
  • NPC Circular No. 2023-06 — Minimum Security Requirements
  • NPC Advisory No. 2023-01 — Guidelines on Deceptive Design Patterns
  • NPC Advisory No. 2024-01 — Model Contractual Clauses for Cross-Border Transfers
  • Republic Act No. 10175 — Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012
Important Notice Gumi Save is a household budget recording tool only — it has no payment processing, financial transaction, or wagering features. No money is transferred through this app.

Section 03

Personal Data We Collect

Gumi Save collects only the minimum data necessary to operate the service (data minimization principle).

3.1 Facebook Login Data

When you log in using Facebook, we receive only the following data that you have authorized in your Facebook privacy settings:

DataPurpose
Profile name (display name)To identify and display your account
Email addressAccount creation, login, and service notifications
Birthday (date of birth)Age-appropriate service delivery per Facebook's API

We do not access your Facebook friends list, posts, messages, likes, or any other Facebook data.

Optional Facebook Sharing Feature If you choose to share a savings milestone or budget achievement via the in-app sharing feature, the App will request your explicit permission before posting to your Facebook timeline. This feature is entirely opt-in — you will always see a preview of what will be posted and must actively confirm each post. You may revoke Facebook posting permission at any time through Facebook → Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites → Gumi Save → Permissions.

3.2 Household Budget Data

Data you voluntarily enter into the app:

  • Income and expense records (amounts, dates, categories)
  • Budget envelope allocations and balances
  • Savings goals, target amounts, and progress notes
  • Transaction descriptions and personal notes
  • Receipt images (only when you voluntarily use the receipt scanning feature; images are processed locally or via OCR for text extraction and are not stored permanently after processing)

3.3 What We Do NOT Collect

  • Precise GPS location
  • Device contacts or call logs
  • SMS messages
  • Bank account numbers, credit card numbers, or other payment credentials
  • GCash or Maya account credentials, passwords, or PINs
  • Any data from other apps on your device
  • Device identifiers beyond what is required for push notifications
All financial data in Gumi Save is self-reported by you. We do not currently connect to any bank, remittance service, or payment system, and no actual money moves through this app.

Planned GCash / Maya Integration: A future version is planned to include optional GCash and Maya account linking. If introduced, a separate, updated Privacy Policy and explicit opt-in consent will be presented before any payment account data is accessed.

3.4 Technical Data (Automatically Collected)

  • App version number
  • Operating system type and version (Android/iOS)
  • Crash logs and error reports (anonymized, for bug fixing only)
  • Language preference

Device Permissions: Gumi Save requests camera permission only when you use the receipt scanning feature. Camera access is never activated without your action. You may revoke camera permission at any time through your device settings (Settings → Apps → Gumi Save → Permissions → Camera).

Section 09

Data Security

Our security measures comply with NPC Circular No. 2023-06 (Minimum Security Requirements):

Technical Measures

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Database storage is encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely and rotated regularly
  • Strict role-based access controls (RBAC) limit who within KawKaw.dev can access user data

Organizational Measures

  • Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA) is conducted before implementing new data processing activities
  • Staff handling personal data are trained on data privacy obligations
  • Vendor contracts (DPAs) are maintained for all sub-processors

Incident Management — Personal Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach, we will follow this mandatory two-step procedure:

Step 1 — Within 72 hours of discovery
  • Notify the NPC via the official NPC Data Breach Notification Management System (DBNMS) (submissions outside DBNMS are not valid)
  • Notify affected data subjects individually via email
  • If the breach involves 100 or more data subjects, or involves sensitive personal information — no delay, notification is immediate
Step 2 — Within 5 business days
  • Submit a complete detailed breach report to the NPC via DBNMS containing: nature of the breach, personal data involved, number of affected data subjects, remediation steps taken, and designated representative contact details

Annual Reporting: In accordance with NPC requirements, we submit an Annual Security Incident Report (ASIR) via the NPC DBNMS every year, documenting all security incidents including those that did not require immediate breach notification.

Section 12

Facebook Login

When you sign in with Facebook, we receive only the data described in Section 3.1 (profile name, email, birthday) as authorized by your Facebook privacy settings. You can revoke our access at any time through Facebook's App Settings: Facebook → Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites → Gumi Save → Remove.

After revoking Facebook access, your Gumi Save account and budget data remain accessible. Your Facebook login will be deactivated; contact kawkaw.app@gmail.com to set an alternative login method.

To revoke only the posting permission (without removing login): Facebook → Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites → Gumi Save → Permissions → Remove "Publish to your timeline".

Seksyon 03

Personal nga Datos nga Amon Gikolekta

Ang Gumi Save nagkolekta lamang sa pinakagamay nga datos nga gikinahanglan aron mapadagan ang serbisyo (prinsipyo sa data minimization).

3.1 Datos sa Facebook Login

DatosKatuyoan

Email addressPaghimo sa account, pag-log in, ug mga abiso sa serbisyo
Adlaw sa pagkatawoPaghatag sa serbisyo base sa edad sumala sa Facebook API

Dili namo ma-access ang imong listahan sa mga higala sa Facebook, mga post, mensahe, likes, o bisan unsang ubang datos sa Facebook.

Opsyonal nga Bahin sa Pagpaambit sa Facebook Kung pilion nimong ipaambit ang usa ka savings milestone, ang App mohangyo sa imong hayag nga pagtugot sa wala pa mag-post sa imong Facebook timeline. Kini nga bahin hingpit nga opt-in. Mahimo nimong bawion ang pagtugot bisan kanus-a pinaagi sa: Facebook → Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites → Gumi Save → Permissions.

3.2 Datos sa Gastos sa Balay

  • Mga rekord sa kita ug gasto (mga kantidad, petsa, kategorya)
  • Mga pag-allocate sa envelope budget ug mga balanse
  • Mga tumong sa pagtipig, target nga kantidad, ug mga nota sa progreso
  • Mga paghulagway sa transaksyon ug personal nga mga nota
  • Mga hulagway sa resibo (sa diha lang nga boluntaryo nimong gamiton ang bahin sa pag-scan)

3.3 Unsa ang DILI Namo Gikolekta

  • Eksakto nga lokasyon sa GPS
  • Mga contact o call log sa device
  • Mga mensahe sa SMS
  • Numero sa bank account, credit card, o bisan unsang kredensyal sa bayad
  • GCash o Maya account credentials, password, o PIN
  • Bisan unsang datos gikan sa ubang mga app sa imong device
  • Mga identifier sa device gawas sa gikinahanglan alang sa mga push notification
Giplano nga Integrasyon sa GCash / Maya: Ang sunod nga bersyon sa app gilaraw nga adunay opsyonal nga pag-link sa GCash ug Maya account. Kung ipaila kini nga bahin, usa ka na-update nga Polisiya sa Pribasidad ug hayag nga opt-in consent ang ipakita kanimo sa wala pa ma-access ang bisan unsang datos sa payment account.

3.4 Teknikal nga Datos (Awtomatiko nga Gikolekta)

  • Numero sa bersyon sa app
  • Uri ug bersyon sa operating system (Android/iOS)
  • Mga crash log ug ulat sa error (anonymized)
  • Kagustuhan sa pinulongan

Seksyon 09

Seguridad sa Datos

Ang among mga lakang sa seguridad nagsunod sa NPC Circular No. 2023-06:

Teknikal nga mga Lakang

  • Ang tanan nga datos sa transit naka-encrypt gamit ang TLS 1.2 o mas taas
  • Ang pagtipig sa database naka-encrypt gamit ang AES-256
  • Mahigpit nga kontrol sa pag-access base sa tungkulin (RBAC)
  • Ang mga authentication token luwas nga gitipigan ug regular nga giilis

Mandatory nga Two-Step nga Pamaagi sa Personal Data Breach

Lakang 1 — Sulod sa 72 oras gikan sa pagkadiskubre
  • Abisohan ang NPC pinaagi sa opisyal nga NPC DBNMS
  • Abisohan ang mga apektadong data subject sa individual pinaagi sa email
  • Kung naglakip sa 100 o labaw pa ka data subjects o sensitive nga impormasyon — agarang abiso
Lakang 2 — Sulod sa 5 ka adlaw sa negosyo
  • Mag-submit sa kompleto ug detalyadong ulat sa paglabag sa NPC pinaagi sa DBNMS

Tinuig nga Pag-report sa Seguridad: Nagsumite kami og Annual Security Incident Report (ASIR) pinaagi sa NPC DBNMS matag tuig.
