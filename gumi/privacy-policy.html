Gumi Save - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 3, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Welcome to Gumi Save ("the App"), a personal finance management application developed and operated by KawKaw.dev ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information in accordance with the Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012) of the Philippines and its Implementing Rules and Regulations.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Stored Only on Your Device (Local Storage)

The majority of your data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly initiate a backup or use an online feature. This includes:

  • Budget envelopes, transactions, income, and expense records
  • Savings goals (Dreams) and progress
  • Debt tracking records (Utang/Hulogan) and payment schedules
  • Utility bill calculations and history
  • Quick expense templates
  • App settings (language, theme, budget period)
  • App lock PIN (stored as encrypted hash)

1.3 Information Collected When Using Online Features

Certain features require an internet connection and temporarily process your data. These features are clearly marked with an "online" badge in the App:

  • Receipt Scanning: Your receipt photo is sent to Google Cloud Vision API for text extraction, then to Google Gemini AI for expense categorization. Images are processed in real-time and are not stored on third-party servers.
  • AI Coach: Your transaction summary and monthly financial data are sent to Google Gemini AI to generate financial coaching advice. Only aggregated summary data is sent, not individual transaction details.
  • Palm Reading (Palad Guide): Your palm photo is sent to Google Gemini AI for entertainment-only palm reading. Images are processed in real-time and not stored.
  • Daily Fortune (Daily Swerte): Your profile information (birthday, gender, zodiac sign, birth hour, region) is sent to generate your daily fortune.
  • Facebook Connect: If you choose to link your Facebook account, we request access to your public profile and email address (via the public_profile and email OAuth scopes). We receive your Facebook user ID, name, email, and profile picture URL. This data is used for account linking and upcoming extended features (such as viewing your Facebook friends who also use the App, and verifying your identity for in-app events and promotions), and is stored in our authentication system (Supabase Auth). Facebook linking is not required to use the App and does not function as an app login method. We do not post on your Facebook or access your posts, photos, or other Facebook data beyond the basic profile information listed above.

1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not use any analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not track your location

6. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • Sensitive data (credentials, PIN, profile) is encrypted using your device's secure storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android)
  • Screen capture prevention is enabled to protect your financial data
  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Server-side data is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies — only you can access your own data
  • Backup files are compressed (gzip) and stored securely with access-controlled signed URLs
  • App lock with PIN and optional biometric authentication

7. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to affect your rights and freedoms, we will:

  • Notify the National Privacy Commission (NPC) within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by the Data Privacy Act of 2012 and NPC Circular 2016-03
  • Notify affected users within a reasonable period through email (if registered) or through an in-app announcement
  • Provide details about the nature of the breach, the data involved, and the measures taken to address it

8. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local data: Remains on your device until you delete the App or clear app data
  • Backup files: Automatically deleted from our servers within 1 to 3 months
  • Server-stored data: AI Coach reports and Daily Fortune results are retained until overwritten or until you request deletion
  • Facebook data: If you linked your Facebook account, you may disconnect it at any time from the App's Settings screen. Upon disconnection, all Facebook-related data (Facebook user ID, name, email, profile picture URL) is immediately and permanently deleted from our authentication system. Your financial data and app records are not affected by Facebook disconnection.
  • Facebook-initiated removal: If you remove Gumi Save from your Facebook account settings, we receive a deauthorization callback from Facebook and automatically delete all your Facebook identity data from our servers.
  • Selective deletion (local): From Settings > Safe & Secure > Clear Records by Month, you may permanently delete transaction records for specific month ranges while keeping the rest of your data. From My Utang List > Clear history of completed, you may clear the borrowed/lent amounts and hulugan/collected history of fully paid or fully collected utangs. These actions affect local data only and cannot be undone.
  • Complete data wipe: You may perform a complete data wipe from the App's Settings screen (Reset App), which automatically disconnects Facebook (if linked), signs out your session, and erases all local data including financial records, settings, and scheduled notifications.
  • Account deletion: You may request complete deletion of your account and all associated server-side data by submitting the Account Deletion Request Form. Once we receive your request, we will permanently delete all of your data from our servers and notify you by email when the process is complete. We will process your request within 30 days.

9. Your Rights Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012

As a data subject under Philippine law, you have the following rights:

  • Right to be Informed: You have the right to know how your personal data is being collected, used, and processed.
  • Right to Access: You may request access to your personal data that we hold on our servers.
  • Right to Correction: You may request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure or Blocking: You may request the deletion or blocking of your personal data from our servers.
  • Right to Object: You may object to the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request a copy of your data in a commonly used electronic format. The App's backup feature already provides this functionality.
  • Right to File a Complaint: You may file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) if you believe your data privacy rights have been violated.
