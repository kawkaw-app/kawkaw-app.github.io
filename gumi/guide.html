What is Quick Expense?

Quick Expense is a shortcut feature that lets you record frequently used expenses with a single tap from the dashboard. For example, if you ride a jeepney every day for P13, you can set it up once and then just tap it each time.

How to set it up:

  1. Go to Settings (from the side menu).
  2. Scroll down to the Management section.
  3. Tap "Quick Expense".
  4. Tap "+ Add Item" at the bottom.
  5. Fill in the Item Name (e.g., "Jeepney"), choose an Emoji, set the Amount (e.g., P13), and select an Envelope category.
  6. Save. The Quick Expense will now appear on your Dashboard!

How to use it: Simply tap the Quick Expense button (e.g., "Jeepney P13") on the dashboard, and the expense is instantly recorded. You also see a "Manage" link to edit your Quick Expense items.

Note: If you haven't added any Quick Expense items, this section will not appear on the dashboard.

Ano ang Quick Expense?

Ang Quick Expense ay isang shortcut feature na nagbibigay-daan sa iyo na mag-record ng madalas gamitin na gastos sa isang tap lang mula sa dashboard.

Paano i-set up:

  6. I-save. Lalabas na ang Quick Expense sa iyong Dashboard!

Paano gamitin: I-tap lang ang Quick Expense button sa dashboard, at matatala agad ang gastos.

How the Amount Buttons Work (+1, +5, +10...)

Instead of typing the full amount, you tap the quick-add buttons to accumulate the amount. This is especially useful for small daily expenses.

Available buttons: +P1, +P10, +P50, +P100, +P500, +P1k, +P5k, +P10k

  2. Tap +P1 three times (Amount shows: P13)

Hulugan Schedule - Complete Guide

A Hulugan Schedule is a payment plan for paying off your debts in installments. You can set this up for each person/store you owe.

Schedule Types:

  • Weekly: Pay the same amount every week.
  • Biweekly: Pay every two weeks.
  • Monthly: Pay once a month.
  • Manual: Choose specific dates and amounts yourself. Useful for irregular payment plans.

Components:

  • Hulugan Schedule Selector: Choose Weekly, Biweekly, Monthly, or Manual.
  • First Hulugan Date: The date of your first payment.
  • Hulugan Amount: The amount per installment.
  • Manual Mode: Select dates using a calendar picker and enter custom amounts for each date. A running total is shown. The app warns if "The sum of hulugan amounts must equal the total utang amount."
  • Payment Alarm: Toggle ON to receive payment reminders.
    • Alarm Time: Set the time for the reminder notification.
    • Notify me: Choose when to be notified - "On due date", "1 day before", "2 days before", or "3 days before".
  • Last Hulugan Date: Shows when the final payment is scheduled.

Scenario: Setting up a Hulugan Schedule

  1. Go to the Utang tab and find the person/store card.
  2. Tap "Add Hulugan Schedule" on their card.
  3. Choose a schedule type (e.g., Weekly).
  4. Set the first payment date and amount per installment.
  5. Enable Payment Alarm if desired, set the alarm time and notification timing.
  6. Tap Save. The schedule is now active!
  7. On the Utang tab, you'll now see the Hulugan summary showing upcoming payments and the calendar will display payment dates with dots.
  8. When a Hulugan schedule exists, the button changes to "Edit Hulugan Schedule" (green button).

How to Transfer Data to a New Device - Step by Step

When you get a new phone and want to keep all your Gumi Save data, follow these steps carefully:

On Your OLD Device:

  1. Open Gumi Save and go to Settings.
  2. Scroll down to Safe & Secure section.
  3. Tap Backup. Wait for the upload to complete. Your data is now securely stored on the server.
  4. Tap OTP. A one-time password (verification code) will be generated. Write this code down or screenshot it! You will need it on the new device.

On Your NEW Device:

  1. Install and open Gumi Save. Complete the initial onboarding (enter name and PIN).
  2. Go to Settings > Safe & Secure.
  3. Tap Restore.
  4. Enter the OTP code you received from the old device.
  5. Wait for the restore to complete. All your data (envelopes, transactions, utang, dreams, etc.) will be restored!

Important Reminders:

  • Complete the transfer within 30 days of creating the backup! After 30 days, the backup is automatically deleted from the server.
  • The OTP is a one-time use code. If you lose it, you'll need to create a new backup and OTP from the old device.
  • Make sure you have an internet connection on both devices for Backup, OTP, and Restore.

Facebook Connect lets you link your Facebook account to Gumi Save. This is not the same as logging in — Gumi Save does not use Facebook as a login method.   • Event participation: Verify your identity for in-app events, challenges, and promotions.

  • Your Facebook user ID
  • Your Facebook name
  • Your email address (from Facebook)
