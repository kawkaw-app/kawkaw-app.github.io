From the KawKaw team 🌸
We started KawKaw because we saw real people — families, solo earners, students, anyone trying to take better care of themselves — underserved by apps designed in rooms they'd never enter. So we build differently. Small, focused apps for specific audiences. GUMI SAVE for Filipino households learning to budget. GUMI GLOWUP for anyone, anywhere, building a skincare routine they can stick with. More apps will follow — each one made for someone real. Here's what we promise across every app we ship: We'll keep listening. Every message. Every idea. Every "sana may ganito." We won't sell you out. Your data is yours. Full stop. We'll be here tomorrow. And the day after that. Building quietly, improving constantly. Thank you for giving us a chance.
— The KawKaw Team Small team · Big care 🌱
