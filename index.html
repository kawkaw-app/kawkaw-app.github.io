Household Budget App

GUMI SAVE

Your cute & friendly budgeting buddy! Track income, expenses, utang, and savings all in one place. Make budgeting feel like a game, not a chore. 🎮

  • 💵 Track income & expenses easily
  • 📦 Envelope budgeting system
  • 🤝 Utang (debt) tracker with Hulugan schedule
  • 🐷 Dream Board — savings goals with progress
  • 🧾 Scan receipts for quick entry
  • ⚡ Quick Expense — one-tap daily spending
  • 📅 Calendar view for all transactions
  • 💡 Utility Bill Calculator & Estimator
  • 🤖 AI Coach & spending analytics
  • 🏠 Home Screen Widgets
  • 📵 Works 100% offline, anytime

Growing Together

Starting in the Philippines, but dreaming bigger. As we grow, you grow with us — and your feedback guides the way.

March 2026 — From our small team to you 🌸
Dear Ate, Kuya, Manang, Mano, and everyone finding their way, We're not here to get rich quick. We're not here to collect your data. We're not here to disappear after a year. We started KawKaw because we saw real people — families, solo earners, students — struggling with bills they didn't see coming and budgets that slipped away without warning. So we built GUMI SAVE. Simple. Honest. Free. Right now, this app is just a small helping hand — a companion for your everyday budgeting. But if you keep supporting us, sharing your ideas, and helping us grow, we have a bigger dream: to build real financial tools that make a difference — micro-loans, micro-investments, point rewards, and more. A system that truly empowers every Filipino, not just tracks their money. But here's what matters most to us: we will keep listening to you. Every message you send, every idea you share, every "sana may ganito" you mention — we take it seriously. That's not marketing talk. That's our commitment. We might be small today. But we'll be here tomorrow, and the day after that. Building quietly, improving constantly, and always putting you first. Salamat for giving us a chance. 🙏 We promise to make it worth your trust.
