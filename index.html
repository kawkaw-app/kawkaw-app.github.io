Our Apps

Two apps. One simple goal. 🎯

Help every Filipino family take control of their money — without stress, without cost, and without needing the internet.

Utility Bill Tracker

BAYAD

Know your electricity, water, and internet bills before they arrive. BAYAD estimates your monthly utility costs by region — so no more bill shock! 😮

  • ⚡ Predict electricity costs by appliance & kWh
  • 💧 Estimate water bills by region
  • 📶 Track internet plan spending
  • 📍 Localized rates (Metro Manila & more)
  • 🌞 Seasonal alerts — summer? expect higher bills!
  • 📵 Fully offline — no data needed
🆓 100% Free

Why Choose Us

We're different — in a good way 🌱

We don't have a huge office. We don't have investors pushing us. We just want to build apps that truly help.

👂

We Actually Listen

Every feedback you send gets read by a real person. Your ideas shape our next update — not a boardroom decision.

🏘️

Neighborhood Feel

We're like that reliable friend who's always around. No corporate nonsense — just honest, helpful tools for real life.

🆓

Free. Always.

No hidden fees, no premium traps. We believe financial tools should be accessible to everyone, not just those who can pay.

📵

No Internet? No Problem.

We know not everyone has stable data. Both apps work completely offline — because real life doesn't pause for WiFi.

🔒

Your Privacy First

Your financial data never leaves your phone. We don't collect, sell, or peek at anything. Your life is your life.

Growing Together

Starting in the Philippines, but dreaming bigger. As we grow, you grow with us — and your feedback guides the way.

March 2026 — From our small team to you 🌸
Dear Ate, Kuya, Manang, Mano, and everyone finding their way, We're not here to get rich quick. We're not here to collect your data. We're not here to disappear after a year. So we built BAYAD and GUMI SAVE. Simple. Honest. Free. But here's what matters most to us: we will keep listening to you.
